Some words about safety of your Digitalcoins:

  • Digitalcoins in Digitalcoin Wallet are stored on the device. If you lose your device, you'll lose your Digitalcoins. Just like money in your physical wallet.
  • Before uninstalling Digitalcoin Wallet (or clearing app data/wiping your device), transfer your Digitalcoins to another wallet. Any remaining Digitalcoins will be lost.
  • Transactions cannot be undone. If you send your Digitalcoins into the void, there is no way to get them back unless you know the receiver and he/she is willing to send them back.
  • Keep your mobile device safe! Do only install apps you fully trust. Malicious apps could be trying to steal your wallet.
  • Keep the risk low! Only use Digitalcoin Wallet with small amounts for day use, and keep your Digitalcoin savings at a safer place.
